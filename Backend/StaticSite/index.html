Where's Bitly? How to Play

Important: You must have an active Twitter account in order to play Where's Bitly. Only people who are registered, confirmed and have a sticker can play.

Read Full T&Cs.

Follow @BitWhere on Twitter so you can get updates as the day progresses.

Registration

  1. Register by sending a selfie to @BitWhere with #PlayFindBit in the message.


     
  2. Come to the Microsoft stand at the event and we will confirm your registration and give you a sticker to show you are a player.

Playing the game

  1. @BitWhere will announce when a new round begins (a 'Byte') by tweeting, for example "New round beginning! #Byte1".


     
  2. We'll give you a hint of who the new Bit is at the Microsoft stand.
     
  3. Once you think you know who it is, find them and tweet a picture of them to @BitWhere with #IFoundBit and #ByteX in the message where 'X' is the round number (i.e. #Byte1 for round 1).


     

Winning the game

  1. @BitWhere will announce when a winning entry has been received.


     
  2. Come by the Microsoft stand to see if you are the winner and collect your prize!
